Narnia

  • Origin: "The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe" (1950)
  • Population: N/A (due to the destruction of Narnia in the year 2555, Narnia technically no longer exists)

Geography and Cosmology

The country of Narnia is the primary location of C.S. Lewis's "The Chronicles of Narnia". According to the series' myths, Narnia was created by Aslan, the great lion, and populated by talking animals and mythical creatures alike. Narnia features rolling hills rising into the low mountain range in the south, and is predominantly forested except for the northern marshlands. It is bordered by the Eastern Ocean to the east, the River Shribble to the north, a big mountain range to the west, and the country of Archenar to the south.
